💻 USAGE INSTRUCTIONS:

To activate the autonomous training system:

```python
from improved_dual_personality_chatbot import ImprovedDualPersonalityChatbot

# Initialize chatbot with autonomous training capabilities
bot = ImprovedDualPersonalityChatbot()

# Enable continuous autonomous training
bot.enable_autonomous_training()

# Monitor training progress
status = bot.get_autonomous_training_status()
print(f"Training sessions: {status['sessions_completed']}")
print(f"Current accuracy: {status['current_accuracy']}%")
print(f"Improvement rate: {status['improvement_rate']:+.1f}%")

# Get training recommendations
recommendations = bot.get_training_recommendations()
for rec in recommendations:
    print(f"• {rec}")

```
